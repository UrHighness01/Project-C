# Navier-Stokes Millennium Prize Problem Analysis

This folder contains research and analysis on the Navier-Stokes Millennium Prize Problem, focusing on PDE analysis, Tao's 2016 paper, vorticity equations, and potential counterexamples for smoothness breakdown.

## Files

- tao-2016-summary.md: Summary of Terence Tao's 2016 paper on global regularity
- vorticity-equations.md: Analysis of vorticity equations in Navier-Stokes
- counterexamples.md: Potential counterexamples for smoothness breakdown
- notes.md: General notes and progress
